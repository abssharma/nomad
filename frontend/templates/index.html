why nomad?

every day, countless lives go unheard. people lost to systems, missing from families, and forgotten by society. nomad gives them a voice—a chance to be seen, heard, and helped.

who can use it?

  • police — identify missing persons and possibilty to reunite them with loved ones.
  • ngo — access mental health insights from transcribed interviews or audio clips.
  • social workers — view location-based data on homelessness patterns.
  • humanitarians & volunteers — understand and act on the needs of those forgotten.

everybody likes voiceless robots but what about humans?

nomad empowers, tracks neglected individuals, and builds bridges back to society. this is data with a heartbeat.

a promise to listen. to find. to care. because every life matters. every voice counts.
